Starting from the basic level of sensory perception all the way to comprehensive cosmic consciousness, we see a logical sequence in the development of cognitive capabilities. The idea of the pyramid is borrowed from the structure of ancient pyramids in many cultures, which represent the ascent from the earthly to the spiritual. This hierarchical model of human and civilizational cognitive evolution has a clear sequential flow and builds upon previous levels in order to rise. The model emphasizes the importance of a solid foundation for cognitive development.

This model presents the following:

1.  **Physical Base**: This represents the material and technological foundation of civilization, the base upon which all other levels are constructed, signifying the importance of physical and technological infrastructure. This includes the material resources, tools, and technology that support human activity, and forms the fundamental platform for society.
2.  **Cognitive Middle**: This encompasses human scientific, philosophical, and cultural knowledge across all eras into the future, the central component supporting the overall structure, which highlights the importance of knowledge. This layer includes all forms of human understanding, as well as all creative expression. This layer is critical for the growth and advancement of civilization.
3.  **Sensory/Spiritual Peak**: This represents the various levels of consciousness and perception of all entities, the highest point of cognitive evolution, demonstrating the importance of spiritual awareness. This layer encompasses the higher dimensions of human understanding, and includes insights from higher levels of cognition and perception. This is the apex of human cognitive and spiritual development.
