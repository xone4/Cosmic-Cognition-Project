This hierarchical model of human and civilizational cognitive evolution has a clear sequential flow and builds upon previous levels in order to rise.

This model presents the following:

1.  **Physical Base**: This represents the material and technological foundation of civilization, the base upon which all other levels are constructed, signifying the importance of physical and technological infrastructure.
2.  **Cognitive Middle**: This encompasses human scientific, philosophical, and cultural knowledge across all eras into the future, the central component supporting the overall structure, which highlights the importance of knowledge.
